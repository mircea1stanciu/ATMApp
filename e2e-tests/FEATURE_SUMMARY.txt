✨ WHAT'S NEW:

  📸 SCREENSHOTS FOR SUCCESS MESSAGES
     • Automatically captures screenshots after each successful auth test
     • Includes: Super Admin, Org Admin, Regular User, Logout, Session Refresh
     • Timestamped filenames for easy identification
     • Stored in: test-results/screenshots/

  🎥 VIDEO RECORDING
     • Configurable video recording for test flows
     • Default: Records only on test failures (efficient)
     • Optional: Record all tests with PLAYWRIGHT_VIDEO=always
     • Format: WebM (universally supported)
     • Stored in: test-results/videos/

  📊 ENHANCED REPORTING
     • HTML reports include embedded screenshots
     • Video thumbnails in reports
     • Console logging of all captures
     • JSON results for CI/CD integration
